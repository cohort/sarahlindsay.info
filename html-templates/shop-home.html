The Dust Collection

Scroll to shop

The Dust Collection is made of thin, translucent slices of acrylic. I make the material from acrylic particles which is why the collection is called 'Dust'. It suggests change and the eroded looking edges of the slices are reminiscent of minerals like agate, built up in layers over time.

I want to imitate nature, looking at thin, translucent natural structures such as leaves, petals, butterfly wings and honesty seeds. Colour is suspended in an outline with fine inner lines. Contrasting matt polyester colour with translucent acrylic gives designs a contemporary edge.
